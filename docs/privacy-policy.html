Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Welcome to AutoAI - Auto Intelligence ("AutoAI", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using AutoAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and role (DIYer or Mechanic) when you register
  • Profile Information: Profile photo and any information you add to your profile
  • User Content: Posts, comments, photos, and vehicle information you share on the platform
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle details and maintenance logs you enter
  • Communications: Messages and interactions with other users

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, app crashes, and performance data

Information from Third Parties

  • Firebase: We use Firebase for photo storage and authentication services
  • AI Services: Car diagnosis queries are processed through Anthropic's Claude AI API
  • YouTube: We display YouTube videos through the YouTube Data API

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide AI-powered car diagnosis and recommendations
  • Display relevant repair videos and community content
  • Enable social features including posts, comments, likes, and follows
  • Process and display profile photos and vehicle information
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • Communicate with you about updates and important notices
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Voice Recognition

AutoAI offers optional voice input for car diagnosis queries. When you use voice features:

  • Your device's microphone is accessed only when you actively tap the voice button
  • Speech is processed locally on your device and/or through your device's speech recognition service
  • We do not store audio recordings
  • You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your posts, profile, and public activity are visible to other AutoAI users
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate the app (Firebase, Anthropic, YouTube)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • JWT token-based authentication
  • Firebase secure cloud storage for media files

While we implement these safeguards, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
